Vision:

An undependent and progressive barangay who is an advocate of
the principles of good governance that help build and nurture
honesty and responsibility among its public officials and
employees and take appropriate measures to promote
transparency in transacting with the constituency.

Mission:

To be able to actively carry out the
mandate and ensure transparency,
honesty and efficiency in the delivery
of services in the barangay.
